hi!

I am a Master's student in the Cheriton School of Computer Science at the University of Waterloo. I work with Prof. Semih Salihoglu on Graphflow, a single-node in-memory native graph database system. I look at scaling Graphflow in a single node environment dealing with aspects like different data storage models, data compression schemes and index compressions techniques. The intriguing idea is to scale-up as rigorously as possible and ultimately strike a sweet spot of optimum performance and memory.


On a more general note, my interests in Computer Science touches a broad spectrum which includes Machine Learning, Natural-language processing, Compilers and Large-scale systems designing other than Database Systems.
